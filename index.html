The Lord Of The Git

When the great foe Iteration 5 destroyed the very last light of hope, Team 4 resisted. And a legion of software developers went to merge. They left their happiness, and journeyed to a distant realm. One filled with untold conflicts, and strange errors beyond reason. A place known, as Visual Studio.

They said it would be over quickly, but the merge left Team 4 in ruin. And the pain and trauma suffered would last forever. They had learned many words for defeat. In the end, the code would be defeated, but not before compilation. For the documentation was incomplete, with diagrams everywhere. Multiplying ever greater, under the command of the code's most devoted servant. The cruel and cunning application. They called it...PowerDesigner

An analyst vowed to seek and defeat Iteration 5, but Iteration 5 found her first.
